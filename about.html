FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order? +

Visit the Order Tracking page, enter your Order ID (found in your confirmation email), and you'll see real-time updates on your shipment's journey.

What is the return policy? +

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy on all products. Simply initiate a return from your profile page and we'll arrange a free pickup within 2-3 business days. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Are all products genuine and authentic? +

Absolutely. Every product listed on Mazo Deals is sourced directly from authorized manufacturers or brand-certified distributors. We conduct regular quality audits and have a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeits.

How does free shipping work? +

Free shipping is available on all orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, a flat shipping fee of ₹99 is applied. Express delivery options (1-2 days) are also available at an additional charge of ₹149.

Can I cancel my order after placing it? +

Yes! Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement for a full refund. Once the item is packed, cancellation may not be possible, but you can initiate a return once it's delivered.

How do I become a seller on Mazo Deals? +

We'd love to partner with you! Send us an email at partnerships@mazodeals.in with your brand details, product catalogue, and pricing. Our partnerships team will get back to you within 48 hours.
